# Word Scrambler
A digital "Cut-up" machine inspired by William S. Burroughs. 
Break the logic of your sentences and spark new inspirations through chaos.

## 🚀 Concept: Beyond Linear Thinking
We often get stuck in our own patterns of speech and thought. Word Scrambler is a tool designed to shatter those patterns. By physically scattering words across a digital canvas, it forces your brain to see connections and phrases you never would have imagined.

## ✨ Features
- **Chaos Engine:** Unlike standard shufflers, this tool scatters your words randomly across the entire screen—tilted, overlapped, and mirrored.
- **Mirroring Logic:** 25% of words are flipped horizontally. This "unreadable" element adds a layer of raw visual chaos to stimulate your subconscious.
- **High-Speed Scrambling:** Watch your words vibrate and blur during the shuffle, creating a visceral sense of "mixing" before the final landing.
- **One-Tap Re-edit:** Simply tap the chaos canvas to return to the editor. Refine your list and scramble again instantly.
- **Bedside/Cafe Friendly:** High-contrast design optimized for brainstorming sessions anywhere, from a dark room to a bright cafe.

## 🛠 How to Use
1. **Input:** Type or paste words/sentences (one per line) into the editor.
2. **Scramble:** Hit the "SCRAMBLE IT" button.
3. **Behold:** Observe the scattered fragments. Look for accidental juxtapositions that feel right.
4. **Edit:** Tap the screen to go back, add more words, or try a different set.

## 📐 The Philosophy
Following the "1 Tool / 1 Action" mantra of the Fridge Combo project, this tool doesn't analyze your text—it destroys it. It is a "toy for the mind," meant for poets, copywriters, and anyone looking to escape the mundane order of language.
